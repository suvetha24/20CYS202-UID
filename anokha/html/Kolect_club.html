Kolect-knowledge connect

Description:


Kolect is a knowledge connect platform for students to connect and share the ever-growing knowledge from collecting hobbies.

Collecting Hobbies brings in a lot of value addition to individuals, from time management to knowledge sharing. In the era of the digital-virtual world, collectible hobbies shall bring in the required detachment from social media and digital gadgets. Through this club, we would bring back the Collecting Hobbies to our university and promote them by providing a platform to showcase their collectibles and expert sessions.
