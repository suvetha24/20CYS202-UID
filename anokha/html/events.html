CODECODE



Teams are required to unravel real-life issues through coding. Participants should tackle the most significant conceivable questions in indicated time interim in the most proficient way. The programming aptitudes of members would be tested in this competition.

The top 5 teams from each Zonal Qualifier will qualify for the Grand Finale to be held during Techfest 2022-23.

FIND THE LAG



With the input lag being an essential factor for gamers, we can safely say that every millisecond counts. The lower the lag, the faster the response to your actions, setting the targeted value between 50-75 ms. Anything over 80 ms is considered to be inadequate.

With this in mind, participating teams are expected to design an ML Model/ any other software/ innovative techniques measuring the lag between the input through the mouse and output on the screen.

Techfest Olympiad Online



Techfest Olympiad presents students with a platform to develop and showcase their scientific and practical life problem-solving skills. All participants have to solve the maximum number of questions in a specified time interval to compete with the brightest minds of the country. Only Standard 8th to 12th students are eligible to participate.
